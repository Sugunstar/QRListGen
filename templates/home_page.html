QR Code Generator for Lists

Welcome to my project! This innovative solution allows you to generate customized QR codes for lists of items, contacts, or any collection of data. Easily convert your lists into scannable QR codes that can store information efficiently. Perfect for inventory management, event check-ins, or sharing multiple pieces of information simultaneously. The generated QR codes can be scanned by any smartphone device, providing instant access to the stored list in a user-friendly format.
